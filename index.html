I bring ethnographic depth to understanding technology adoption. I don't just collect data about how people use tools—I embed myself in their actual contexts: homes, offices, buses, classrooms, memorial services. I'm trained to see gaps between what people say and what they do, to surface whose perspectives get excluded from systems, and to understand how the structure of a platform shapes behaviour in ways users don't always recognise. I translate qualitative findings into actionable insights about design, strategy, and service decisions.
